about

Hi, this is Marco Rosella: welcome to vlog.it, a random HTML5-powered (with SVG animations) rotating collection of my 313 Vimeo Likes and 1011 YouTube Favorites.

Instructions: Click on a moving slice, enjoy stuff. Switch between Vimeo and Youtube videos under the logo.

More info on this blog post; follow the updates here:

Thanks to Federico Orrù for the server side system and to all the videos' authors.

This project has been named as finalist for the 15th SXSW Interactive Awards in the Motion Graphics category, won the Awwwards Site of the Day and has been featured in countless design galleries.
